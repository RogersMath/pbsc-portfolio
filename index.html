An arcade-style game teaching number theory through gameplay. Shoot red composite-numbered asteroids to break them into their prime factors, and collect blue prime-numbered asteroids. Learn mathematical concepts through action and strategy.

About This Work

These projects represent an exploration of how AI-assisted development can amplify educational impact. As a Learning Lab Coordinator with 20 years of education experience, I've combined pedagogical expertise with modern development tools to create resources that would traditionally require large teams and extended timelines.

Each project demonstrates a different approach to learning: some teach through direct practice, others through strategic gameplay, and still others through reflection and metacognition. Together, they form a portfolio of evidence-based educational interventions accessible to students at Palm Beach State College and beyond.

All game projects are open source and available for adaptation, forking, and community development under MIT licenses.
